Banana Cinnamon Pancakes

Description

These pancakes give a healthy twist to a classic breakfast favourite. Sweetened naturally by the bananas and honey, there are no unwanted nasties in these tasty treats, and they're also full of protein and good carbs and fats. Yummy!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sunflower seeds
  • 1/4 cup chia seeds
  • 1 cup buckwheat
  • 1 teaspoons of ground cinnamon
  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 2 teaspoons of raw honey
  • 1 1/2 cups of milk
  • 2 eggs (lightly beaten)
  • 2 1/2 medium bananas

Procedure

  1. In a bowl, mix the seeds, flours, cinnamon and baking powder.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix the honey, milk, eggs and bananas.
  3. And the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients.
  4. Melt some butter in a medium frying pan over medium heat. ladle in the pancake mixture and flip once sizzling.
  5. Once both side are cooked, repeat this process for the remaining mixture. Serve and enjoy!
