Easy Pancake

Description

A delicious and easy pancake recipe that is fast to get on the table and better tasting than any convenience pre-packaged mix.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup self-raising flour(sifted)
  • 1-2 teaspoons caster sugar
  • 1 egg (lightly beaten)
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 50g butter (melted for frying)

Procedure

  1. In a bowl, beat the egg and then whisk in the milk. Whisk in the sugar (how much you add will depend on how sweet you like your pancakes - don't forget to take toppings into consideration).
  2. Sift the flour and whisk until batter is smooth and lump-free.
  3. In a hot pan over medium-low heat, brush butter over cooking surface and pour 1/4 cup measures for a pancake.
  4. When large bubbles form on the surface, flip the pancake over and cook until lightly golden on the other side. Keep and eye as they do cook quikly!
  5. This recipe makes 8 pancakes.
